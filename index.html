Keep Our Rivers Alive

Rivers are the lifeblood of our planet. They provide water, sustain wildlife, and connect communities. Today, they need our protection more than ever.

Join The Movement

Why Rivers Matter

💧

Freshwater Source

Rivers provide the primary source of drinking water for billions of people and sustain global agriculture.

🐟

Biodiversity Hubs

Freshwater ecosystems harbor critical biodiversity, supporting countless species of fish, birds, and plants.

🌱

Climate Regulation

Healthy rivers and their surrounding wetlands absorb carbon, mitigate floods, and stabilize local climates.

The Rising Crisis

Our Rivers Are in Danger

Human activities have pushed freshwater ecosystems to the brink. Neglect and exploitation are turning vibrant lifelines into toxic channels.

  • Plastic Pollution: Millions of tons of waste clog waterways annually.
  • Chemical Runoff: Industrial waste and agricultural fertilizers poison aquatic life.
  • Over-allocation: Excessive water extraction causes major rivers to dry up before reaching the sea.
